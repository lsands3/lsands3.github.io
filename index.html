Introduction

my name is logan sands. i am a second year computational media major at georgia institute of technology with a background in literary studies and social psychology . hopefully, by next semester, i will declare my cm concentraions to be people and interaction design . i am heavily inspired by music, photography, internet culture, and computer design/editing/graphics etc . i want to go into ui/ux (blended with some form of graphic design) because it is a field that works to improve the connection between people and technology . i want to express my creativity and prowess through coding in a way that not only engages users but inspires them to delve into the world of computational media .

Fun Facts About Maggie

  • Her Birthday is April 25th (3 Days Before Mine) which makes us both Tauruses!
  • She graduated from New York University after being discovered by Pharrell in a master class for the Clive Davis Institute of Recorded Music.
  • In 2020, she was nominated for her first Grammy for Best New Artist. She was robbed.

One of my FAVORITE moments

On November 3rd, 2018, she performed her song "Fallingwater" on Saturday Night Live. Not only was this perfromance great, in a way, it represented her much deserved breakthrough into mainstream media.
