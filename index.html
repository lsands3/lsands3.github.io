By: Logan Sands

Introduction

This page was created in February of 2020. At this point in my life, I am in love with an album called "Heard It In A Past Life" by one of my new artists, Maggie Rogers. I'm not completely sure why, but for some reason, I just feel like I connect with her and her music in a deep way. There is something so raw and honest about her lyrics discussing love, life, emotional depth, and the modern human condition. She has such a unique and admirable perspective and point of view in her writing and that is seen from the first track to the final seconds of this breathtaking album. That is why I have created this page. I am hereby declaring my love and appreciation for her not only as an artist, but as a human being too.

Fun Facts About Maggie

  • Her Birthday is April 25th (3 Days Before Mine) which makes us both Tauruses!
  • She graduated from New York University after being discovered by Pharrell in a master class for the Clive Davis Institute of Recorded Music.
  • In 2020, she was nominated for her first Grammy for Best New Artist. She was robbed.

One of my FAVORITE moments

On November 3rd, 2018, she performed her song "Fallingwater" on Saturday Night Live. Not only was this perfromance great, in a way, it represented her much deserved breakthrough into mainstream media.
